About the Tutor

Achanta
Vasundhara

A seasoned educator with over 27 years of experience in shaping young minds through the realm of mathematics.

Having served as a dedicated High School Maths Teacher at Foster Billabong High International School in Hyderabad until 2019, Vasundhara's passion for teaching extends beyond conventional classrooms.

With a commitment to excellence and personalized instruction, Vasundhara is now offering her expertise through tailored math tuition services for the academic year 2026–27. She caters to students from 8th to 12th grade, specializing in providing focused support to 8th, 9th, and 10th graders, as well as senior secondary students pursuing commerce.

Vasundhara's approach is characterized by highly personalized lessons, ensuring ample individual attention to each student under her guidance. Drawing from her extensive experience, she promises not just academic improvement but strives for the best results, surpassing expectations at every turn.

In her hands, students can expect not only to excel in mathematics but also to develop a deep understanding and appreciation for the subject, setting a solid foundation for their academic journey and beyond.
